# Spicy cucumber

## Ingredients

- 1 Cucumber
- 1 tea spoon Chili flakes
- 3 toes Garlic
- 0.5 cup Vinegar
- Salt

## Step

1. Cut the cucumber, remove the middle parts
1. Mix salt and cucumbers and wait for 20 mins to dehydrate the cucumber
